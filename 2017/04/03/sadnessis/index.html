Sadness is

Apr 3, 2017




關於 About

這是一個孤獨的人承受悲傷并渴望走出悲傷的故事。這也許也是你的故事，而悲傷，只是開始。
This is a story about a lonely person in sadness and eager for relief. Maybe it is your story, and sadness is only the beginning.

不容易上手？進入另外一個人的世界從來都不是一件容易的事。但或許也不難，因為每個人的情感都是相通的。
Not so easy to start? Entering another person’s world is not easy indeed. But maybe it’s not that difficult, since emotions are the same to all human beings.

進入此間只需要一顆寧靜的心，世人啊，這對你來說是否已然是太難的事情？
The key to entering this world is nothing but a peaceful heart, people, is that too hard for you?

操作 Operations

  • 在方塊不同的面上滑動來轉動方塊。
    Roll the Cube: Touch different facets of the cube and move.
  • 在其它空白區域滑動來移動鏡頭。
    Roll the camera: Touch the blank place and move.

章节 Chapters

  • 你在自己的世界里獨行 不知道出路在哪裡
    You are wandering around this universe, don’t know where it ends
  • 希望之花開了又謝 從不為你稍作停留
    The flower of hope blooms and withers. Time waits no one
  • 你總是回到過去 與那一去不回的人相遇
    Time rewinds
  • 你執意要為那些破滅的夢想 安排一個個最好的結局
    You persist with a happy ending for those broken dreams
  • 而今你在這寂寥的夜色中 獨飲物是人非的惆悵
    As nothing is the same before, you drink alone in a silent night
  • 你張開雙手 擁抱那片絕望之海
    You stretched out your arms, to embrace the sea of despair
  • 現實與夢境互相映現 誰能分清這是真是假
    Fact or fiction? Realities and dreams loom up one another
  • 我看見紛繁思緒築成的高墻 和墻外閃亮的明天
    I’ve seen a wall formed by scattered thoughts and the bright future beyond the wall
  • 我逃往未知的旅程 卻無法逃出自己的心里
    I try to set myself free by escaping to a journey unknown. However, I’m still trapped in the cocoon of my mind
  • 我看見絕望在我面前退卻 看見人們不再分離 看見夢想得以圓滿
    I’ve seen the despair is fading away, the crowd is no longer being scattered, and the goals are being accomplished
  • 我看見往事歷歷如新 看見它指著離開的路 向我道別

  • 我看見黑暗中的光芒 看見希望之花為我盛開
    Light in the darkness. The flower of hope blooms

    I drew something out of this emptiness

其它 Miscellaneous

實際上，我希望你能自己探索這個世界里的各個細節。好吧，讓我告訴你幾個作為開始。
As a matter of fact, I do hope you can explore every detail in this world. Anyway, I’ll tell you some to start with.

  • 在主界面中點按字條進入對應的關卡。
    When in main screen, touch the title lines to enter respective game levels.
  • 在遊戲界面點按方塊進入遊戲。
    When in game screen, touch the cube to start the game.
  • 尾部末端的顏色代表受傷的劇烈程度，黃色最輕，紅色最嚴重，橙色居於二者之間。
    The color of the end of the tail represents that to what degree the player’s getting hurt, yellow means slight, red means heavy, orange means median.
  • 完成一定進度后會開啟無盡模式關卡。
    Levels of endless mode will be unlocked when you reach certain progress.
  • 背景音乐的名字是《望春风》。
    Hope for spring is the name of the background music.

最後，在黑暗裡，戴上耳機，一個人。
In the end, in the dark, with headphone on, alone.
