Magicube Speed

About

Not so easy to play in a professional magic cube competition? This is the place where the events never end. Come to join cube-fans all over the world! Enjoy it!

GameCenter and Leaderboard

Users authenticated by Apple’s game center can upload their scores to the game center’s leaderboard.
There are 2 leaderboards for every kind of cube:

  • Best of 3
  • Average of 5

Operations

  • Sway the screen in up-down or left-right direction to make camera move in 9 positions totally.
  • Touch the blank place and move to roll the cube wholly.
  • Touch the cube and move to roll the specified part of the cube.

Regulations

Inspection

At the beginning of each attempt, you have 15 seconds to inspect the cube, during this period you can only roll the cube wholly, press both hand-shaped buttons to start the solving.

DNF

Did Not Finish, DNF is the worst result of a game. Retiring during a single attempt cause DNF, while spending more than 1 hour during a single attempt in a competition also cause DNF.

Average of 5

The best and worst results during 5 attempts will be removed first, the average of the remaining 3 results will be the final result of the competition. If there’re more than 1 DNF in 5, the final result will be DNF.

Best of 3

The best result in 3 attempts will be the final result of the competition. If all the results are DNFs, the final result will be DNF.

Redo an attempt

An attempt solved in less than 4 moves will be considered invalid and one more attempt will be given.

Thanks

Rules in this game are derived from WCA(World Cube Association).

issues

If you notice that the small rolling cube go on without disappearing, it suggests you are waiting for the game center authenticating in a bad network condition, just quit and logout the game center in Settings of the IOS, or close the internet linking will solve this issue.
